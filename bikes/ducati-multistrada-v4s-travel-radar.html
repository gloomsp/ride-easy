About the Travel Radar Model

Sharing the same 1,158 cc V4 Granturismo engine as the Full model, the Travel Radar trim adds the optional radar package as standard. This system uses front and rear sensors to enable adaptive cruise control and blind‑spot monitoring, helping you maintain a safe following distance and alerting you to vehicles in your blind spots. With 170 hp on tap and silky smooth power delivery, it’s as exciting to ride as it is comfortable.

Ducati pairs the radar tech with electronic suspension, adjustable ride modes, cornering ABS and traction control. Rear‑cylinder deactivation helps keep temperatures down in city traffic, while the 6.5‑inch TFT dash integrates navigation and Bluetooth connectivity. Longer service intervals mean you spend more time exploring and less time in the workshop.
