About the Multistrada V2

Ducati’s V2 replaces the long‑running 950 model with a machine that’s easier to handle yet just as capable. Its 937 cc Testastretta 11° L‑twin produces around 115 hp and 68 lb‑ft of torque. According to ADV Pulse, the V2 is 11 lb lighter than its predecessor thanks to a revised engine, wheels and swingarm【342951754805790†L59-L90】.

The bike features four power modes, adjustable suspension and a suite of electronics including cornering ABS, traction control and wheelie control. Riders will appreciate the accessible 32 in seat height and the optional low seat, making it friendly for a wide range of riders. A six‑axis IMU allows the V2 to adjust power delivery based on lean angle, giving confidence on twisty roads【342951754805790†L73-L117】.

Whether you’re commuting, touring or exploring backroads, the Multistrada V2 offers versatility and refinement.
