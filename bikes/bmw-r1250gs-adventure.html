About the R1250 GS Adventure

BMW’s R1250 GS Adventure builds on decades of globe‑trotting heritage. Its 1,254 cc boxer twin uses ShiftCam variable valve timing to deliver strong power across the rev range, producing about 136 hp and 143 Nm of torque. The unmistakeable boxer thrum and tall stance promise both character and capability.

Electronic aids help tame the terrain: riders can switch between Rain, Road and Enduro ride modes; Dynamic Pro mode unlocks the full potential; and Dynamic Brake Control and Hill Start Control inspire confidence when conditions get tricky. A full LED headlight and large 30‑litre fuel tank make it ready for day‑long journeys on or off the beaten path.
