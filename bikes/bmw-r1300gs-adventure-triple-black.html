About the Adventure Triple Black

The Adventure Triple Black shares its mechanicals with the standard R1300 GS but adopts a striking blacked‑out aesthetic and a larger fuel tank for extended range. The 1,300 cc boxer twin still makes around 145 hp and 110 lb‑ft of torque, while the new frame and 26‑pound weight reduction improve agility over the outgoing 1250 model.

Premium electronics include multiple ride modes, Dynamic Suspension Adjustment, radar‑assisted active cruise control and a 6.5‑inch TFT with integrated navigation and Bluetooth. The Adventure package adds engine bars, luggage mounts and wind protection, and the Triple Black finish gives it a stealthy presence.

If you’re looking for an adventure bike that pairs long‑distance ability with understated style, the Adventure Triple Black should be top of your list. Learn more about BMW’s new GS range in this article, then reserve your ride below.
