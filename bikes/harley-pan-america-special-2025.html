About the Pan America Special

The Pan America Special is powered by Harley‑Davidson’s Revolution Max 1250 engine, delivering about 150 horsepower and strong midrange torque. This liquid‑cooled V‑twin is a structural component of the chassis, helping reduce weight and lower the centre of gravity.

A suite of electronics includes semi‑active suspension with Adaptive Ride Height, multiple ride modes, traction control and cornering ABS. The Special trim also features Brembo brakes, heated grips, a skid plate, hand guards and a 6.8‑inch touchscreen with navigation and Bluetooth. Together these features make it surprisingly capable off‑road while maintaining Harley comfort on long road trips.
