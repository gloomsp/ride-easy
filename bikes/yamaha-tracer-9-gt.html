About the Tracer 9 GT

The Tracer 9 GT uses Yamaha’s 890 cc CP3 triple‑cylinder engine, offering smooth low‑end torque and lively top‑end power. For 2024 Yamaha introduced radar‑assisted adaptive cruise control and radar‑linked unified braking, making it one of the first bikes in its class with this technology. These features help maintain safe distance from traffic and automatically adjust braking in emergency situations.

Semi‑active suspension, an up/down quickshifter and a new 7‑inch TFT display with integrated navigation and smartphone connectivity enhance the riding experience. Heated grips, a height‑adjustable screen and standard panniers make it a true all‑weather tourer.
