About the Adventure Trophy

This Adventure variant of the R1300 GS is designed for riders who crave extended journeys. It features the same punchy 1,300 cc boxer engine (around 145 hp and 110 lb‑ft of torque) paired with a larger fuel tank and enhanced wind protection for days in the saddle. Weight savings from the new steel and aluminium frame keep handling composed off‑road.

Dynamic Suspension Adjustment reacts to changing terrain in milliseconds, while radar‑based active cruise control and collision warnings help manage highway miles. Cross‑spoke wheels, engine guards and auxiliary lights complete the trophy‑ready package. Onboard technology includes a 6.5‑inch TFT screen with connectivity and navigation.

Built for adventurers who plan to tackle deserts, mountains and everything in between, the R1300 GS Adventure Trophy offers uncompromising range and capability. Learn more about BMW’s latest GS innovations in this overview, then check availability below.
