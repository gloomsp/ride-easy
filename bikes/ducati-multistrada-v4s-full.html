About the Multistrada V4 S Full

The Multistrada V4 S is Ducati’s flagship adventure‑tourer. At its heart is the 1,158 cc V4 Granturismo engine that produces around 170 horsepower and 91 lb‑ft of torque. Long service intervals (oil changes every 9,000 miles and valve checks every 37,000 miles) make this V4 a sensible choice for big mileage. The full‑package trim brings a suite of luxury touches including electronic suspension and comprehensive riding aids.

Technology is where the Multistrada truly shines. It features radar‑based adaptive cruise control and blind‑spot monitoring for safer highway travel, multiple ride modes to tailor power delivery for wet or off‑road conditions, and rear‑cylinder deactivation to reduce heat at low speeds. With this level of sophistication it behaves just as comfortably on a long‑distance tour as it does carving mountain roads.
