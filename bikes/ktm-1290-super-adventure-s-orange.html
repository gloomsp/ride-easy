About the 1290 Super Adventure S (Orange)

Like its black‑clad sibling, the orange version of KTM’s 1290 Super Adventure S features the 1,301 cc LC8 V‑twin with around 160 hp and 102 lb‑ft of torque. KTM’s semi‑active suspension and advanced electronics give it the versatility to tackle anything from twisty mountain roads to high‑speed autoroutes.

Adaptive cruise control, multiple ride modes, Motorcycle Traction Control and Offroad mode allow you to tailor the bike to conditions, while options like quickshifter+, Hill Hold Control and a comprehensive tech pack make long rides effortless. The eye‑catching orange paint proudly displays KTM’s racing heritage.
