About the Tiger 900 GT Pro

The Tiger 900 GT Pro is Triumph’s road‑focused adventure bike. Its new 888 cc “T‑plane” triple engine delivers more power and torque than the outgoing Tiger 800 – roughly 106 hp and 66 lb‑ft of torque according to Cycle World’s first ride【223419325681510†L55-L116】. The unusual firing order gives it a gruff sound and punchy low‑rpm response, while maintaining the smoothness Triumph triples are known for.

Riders can dial in their experience with Rain, Road, Sport, Off‑Road and optional Rider modes, plus cornering ABS and traction control that adjust based on lean angle【223419325681510†L55-L116】. The Pro model features electronically adjustable suspension, a full‑colour TFT display, heated seats, cruise control and integrated MyTriumph Bluetooth connectivity. A quickshifter, slipper clutch and up‑down arrow joystick make gear changes effortless.

The Tiger 900 GT Pro strikes a balance between comfort and excitement, equally happy on twisty asphalt and light gravel. It’s a superb choice for riders seeking a lighter alternative to heavyweight adventure bikes. For more insights, see Cycle World’s in‑depth review【223419325681510†L55-L116】.
