About the Triple Black

The heart of the new R1300 GS is a 1,300 cc boxer engine delivering around 145 hp and 110 lb‑ft of torque. The Triple Black edition drapes this advanced machine in a dark, understated colour scheme that emphasises its muscular stance. A redesigned frame and reduced weight improve handling both on tarmac and off‑road.

Advanced electronics abound: radar‑assisted cruise control and collision warnings, multiple riding modes, dynamic suspension and a 6.5‑inch TFT display with full smartphone connectivity. The Triple Black includes premium finishes and textured surfaces for an exclusive look, making it as stylish as it is capable.
