About the 1290 Super Adventure S

KTM’s 1290 Super Adventure S is powered by a 1,301 cc V‑twin delivering around 160 hp and 102 lb‑ft of torque. This prodigious output is managed by a sophisticated electronics package, including multiple ride modes and Motorcycle Traction Control. The chromoly steel frame and long‑travel suspension give it both strength and agility for serious adventures.

WP’s semi‑active suspension adapts damping on the fly, and the radar‑based adaptive cruise control makes long motorway stretches less fatiguing. Options like quickshifter+, Hill Hold Control and a giant 7‑inch TFT dash complete the premium package. The black livery accentuates the aggressive stance while remaining subtle.
