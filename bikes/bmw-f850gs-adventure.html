About the F850 GS Adventure

The F850 GS Adventure is powered by an 853 cc parallel‑twin engine producing about 95 hp and 92 Nm of torque. It’s lighter and more manageable than the big‑bore GS models, yet still offers ample punch for spirited riding on and off road. The Adventure variant comes with a 23‑litre fuel tank for extended range and a more protective fairing.

Standard equipment includes ABS, Automatic Stability Control and traction control, plus two riding modes that adjust throttle response and intervention levels. A 6.5‑inch TFT display with Bluetooth connectivity keeps you informed and connected, while LED lighting improves visibility. Longer‑travel suspension and spoked wheels enhance its off‑road credentials.
