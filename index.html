Why Ride with Us?

Premium Fleet & Coverage

Our fleet includes the latest models from BMW, Ducati, KTM, Triumph, Yamaha and more. We have pick‑up and drop‑off points across Málaga, Torremolinos and the Andalusia region for seamless travel.

Competitive Offers

Enjoy fair prices, seasonal discounts and customised packages for short getaways or long expeditions. Pay only 20% upfront with free cancellation until your trip begins.

Expert Support

From route planning to gear recommendations, our multilingual team provides assistance before and during your ride. We’re motorbike enthusiasts too, and we’ll make sure everything runs smoothly.

Safety First

Your well‑being is our priority. We regularly service our bikes and provide helmets, protective gear and thorough briefing sessions.

What We Offer

Beyond rentals we create unforgettable experiences. Our services include:

  • Guided Tours: Explore Andalusia or cross into Morocco with our expert guides.
  • Self‑Drive Rentals: Flexible rentals for your solo adventures.
  • Morocco Trips: Multi‑day expeditions to the Sahara and Atlas Mountains.
  • Andalusia Getaways: Discover the beauty of southern Spain on two wheels.
  • Deliveries & One‑Way Trips: We can deliver bikes and arrange one‑way routes.
  • Seasonal Discounts: Special offers for long‑term hires and groups.

Group Events & Epic Rides

From stag weekends and corporate retreats to friends celebrating milestones, we love organising large‑group rides. Let our team handle the logistics while you enjoy the open road together.

  • Tailor‑Made Experiences: We design multi‑bike itineraries based on your group’s preferences and skill levels.
  • Support Crew: On bigger trips our support vehicle carries luggage, tools and refreshments so you can focus on riding.
  • Epic Destinations: Choose from popular group routes like Mallorca, Sicily, Scotland, Portugal, Gibraltar or venture to the legendary Dakar.
  • Flexible Options: Add accommodation, catered meals and cultural activities to make your journey truly unforgettable.
